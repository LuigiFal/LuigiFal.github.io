Selected projects

Drifting Equilibrium Rates and Codrifting Yields in the Euro Area

Team project · Bocconi Students Asset Management Club

Empirical project on equilibrium interest rates, monetary policy, and government bond yields. I contributed mainly to the empirical analysis and model implementation.

Sovereign CDS and Bond Spreads

Undergraduate thesis · Bocconi University

Thesis on European sovereign CDS and bond spreads, with a focus on cointegration and price discovery between credit derivatives and bond markets.

Forecasting Denmark’s Real GDP Growth

Group project · Econometrics

Forecasting project comparing dynamic regression models with AR and VAR benchmarks using quarterly macroeconomic data.

Climate Trend and Weather Prediction with State-Space Models

Group project · Time Series Analysis

Project using Hidden Markov Models and Dynamic Linear Models to study climate trends and short-term weather prediction.

Airbnb Expansion and Tourism-Oriented Commerce in Rome

Group project · Applied Econometrics

Empirical project on the relationship between Airbnb listings and tourism-oriented commercial activities in Rome, using panel data and an instrumental-variable approach.

A Bayesian Journey through Dirichlet Processes

Student association project · Mathematical Statistics

Introductory note on Bayesian inference, MCMC methods, and Dirichlet processes, with an application to financial returns.
